How to play

Words will fall down from the top of the box.
Your goal is to type the word before it reaches the bottom.

  • A new word appears in the falling box.
  • Below the box, you see empty letter slots â€“ one for each letter.
  • Start typing on your keyboard:
    • Each letter you type fills the next slot.
    • If a letter is wrong, its box gets a red outline.
    • The word is accepted only when all letters are correct.
  • For every correctly typed word you get +1 score.
  • For every word that reaches the bottom you get -1 score.
  • If you reach -5 score the game is over.

Try to beat your best score!
